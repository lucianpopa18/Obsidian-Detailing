Services

Clear services built for visible improvement.

Every service is easy to scan, easy to understand, and focused on what matters most: what gets done, who it suits, and what kind of result you can expect.

Interior Detailing

Restore the cabin with a full interior clean designed to remove dust, daily grime, and that tired used feel.

Includes
  • Vacuum and air blow-out
  • Dashboard, console, vents, and trims cleaned
  • Interior glass cleaned
  • Light stain treatment
Ideal for: daily drivers, lease returns, and interiors that need a proper reset.

Exterior Detailing

A safe exterior wash and decontamination process that brings back gloss, cleanliness, and a more refined finish.

Includes
  • Safe hand wash and dry
  • Wheel and tire deep clean
  • Bug, tar, and fallout removal
  • Finishing dressings on trims and tires
Ideal for: cars that need more than a fast wash and want a premium look again.

Paint Correction / Polish

Machine polishing to reduce swirl marks, light scratches, haze, and dullness while restoring depth and clarity.

Includes
  • Paint inspection under detailing lights
  • Single-stage or multi-stage correction
  • Gloss enhancement polishing
  • Surface prep for protection
Ideal for: black cars, enthusiast vehicles, and paint that has lost sharpness.

Ceramic Coating

Professional paint protection that enhances gloss, repels contamination, and makes ongoing maintenance easier.

Includes
  • Surface decontamination
  • Panel wipe and coating prep
  • Professional ceramic application
  • Aftercare guidance
Ideal for: owners who want long-term protection and a cleaner-looking car for longer.

Upholstery Cleaning

Deep cleaning for fabric seats, carpets, and mats to lift embedded dirt, spills, and odours.

Includes
  • Fabric pre-treatment
  • Spot stain work
  • Extraction or shampoo process
  • Mat cleaning and drying
Ideal for: family cars, spill-prone interiors, pet hair, and seasonal deep cleans.

Leather Treatment

Clean, nourish, and protect leather surfaces to keep them soft, rich-looking, and properly maintained.

Includes
  • Gentle leather cleaning
  • Crease and seam detailing
  • Conditioning treatment
  • Protective finish
Ideal for: premium interiors, older leather, and cars that deserve a fresher cabin feel.

Headlight Restoration

Restore cloudy headlights for a cleaner appearance and improved clarity at the front of the car.

Includes
  • Oxidation removal
  • Wet sanding when needed
  • Polishing and refinement
  • Protective sealant
Ideal for: faded headlights, older vehicles, and front ends that look tired.

Full Detailing Package

A full inside-and-out transformation for owners who want the most visible all-round improvement in one visit.

Includes
  • Interior deep clean
  • Exterior detail and decontamination
  • Trim, tire, and glass finishing
  • Protection applied to key surfaces
Ideal for: resale prep, special occasions, and cars that need a serious refresh.
