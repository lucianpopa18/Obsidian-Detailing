Services

Professional detailing services built around results.

Every treatment is structured for clarity: what it does, what is included, and who it suits best.

Interior Detailing

A deep interior reset that removes dirt, dust, and daily-use buildup for a fresher cabin.

Includes
  • Full vacuum and blow-out
  • Dashboard, console, vents, and trims cleaned
  • Interior glass cleaned
  • Light stain treatment
Ideal for: daily drivers, lease returns, and neglected interiors.

Exterior Detailing

A careful exterior cleanse that restores gloss, sharpness, and a properly maintained finish.

Includes
  • Safe hand wash
  • Wheel and tire deep clean
  • Bug, tar, and fallout removal
  • Paint-safe drying and finishing dressings
Ideal for: vehicles needing more than a quick wash.

Paint Correction / Polish

Machine polishing designed to reduce swirl marks, oxidation, haze, and light scratches.

Includes
  • Paint inspection under detailing lights
  • Single-stage or multi-stage polish
  • Gloss enhancement
  • Refined finish preparation
Ideal for: dull paint, light defects, and showroom-style gloss.

Ceramic Coating

Long-term paint protection that boosts gloss and makes routine washing easier.

Includes
  • Surface decontamination
  • Panel wipe and coating prep
  • Professional ceramic application
  • Aftercare guidance
Ideal for: owners who want durable protection and easier maintenance.

Upholstery Cleaning

Fabric seats, carpets, and mats are treated to lift embedded dirt, stains, and odours.

Includes
  • Fabric pre-treatment
  • Shampoo or extraction process
  • Mat cleaning
  • Targeted stain work
Ideal for: family cars, spills, pet hair, and seasonal deep cleaning.

Leather Treatment

Leather surfaces are cleaned, nourished, and finished to retain a supple, factory-fresh feel.

Includes
  • Gentle leather cleaning
  • Crease and pore cleaning
  • Conditioning treatment
  • Protective finish
Ideal for: premium interiors, older leather, and routine preservation.

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy or faded headlights are restored for a cleaner appearance and improved visibility.

Includes
  • Surface correction
  • Wet sanding where required
  • Polishing and refinement
  • Protective sealant
Ideal for: oxidized headlights and tired front-end appearance.

Full Detailing Package

A complete inside-and-out transformation for vehicles that need a comprehensive reset.

Includes
  • Interior deep clean
  • Exterior detail and decontamination
  • Trim and tire finishing
  • Protection applied to key surfaces
Ideal for: resale preparation, special occasions, and neglected cars.

Custom Detail Plan

Need a tailored solution? Build a service package based on your vehicle condition and goals.

Includes
  • Condition-based consultation
  • Service recommendations
  • Transparent pricing
  • Optional maintenance plan
Ideal for: performance cars, luxury cars, and specialist care requests.
